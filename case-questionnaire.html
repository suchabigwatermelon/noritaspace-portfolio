招生问卷反馈分析

Turning participant feedback into structured insights for education program improvement and follow-up communication.

01

Project Overview

This case documents how I analyzed participant questionnaire responses from an education program and converted scattered feedback into structured insights for program improvement and follow-up communication.

02

Context

Education programs often collect participant feedback through questionnaires after courses, bootcamps, or training sessions. These responses may include satisfaction ratings, open-ended comments, learning needs, concerns about future participation, and signals related to admissions or follow-up activities.

The raw data is useful, but it is usually fragmented. To make it actionable, the feedback needs to be cleaned, grouped, summarized, and translated into clear findings.

03

Challenge

The challenge was to move beyond simple statistics and identify what the feedback actually meant for future program operations.

The questionnaire responses needed to answer several practical questions: What did participants care about most? Which parts of the program were most valuable? What concerns or questions appeared repeatedly? Were there signals that could support follow-up communication, admissions conversion, or future activity design?

04

My Role

I reviewed the questionnaire data, extracted recurring keywords and themes, grouped participant concerns into clearer categories, and summarized the findings into a short report.

I used AI tools to assist with structuring open-ended responses and drafting summaries, while keeping the final interpretation grounded in the actual program context and operational needs.

05

Process

Four steps from raw responses to insights that could support program decisions.

01

Reviewed raw responses

Collected and reviewed questionnaire responses, including structured options and open-ended feedback.

02

Extracted keywords and themes

Identified recurring concerns, interests, satisfaction points, and follow-up signals from participant comments.

03

Grouped insights by use case

Organized findings into categories related to program improvement, participant needs, and follow-up communication.

04

Summarized for action

Converted the analysis into a concise report that could support internal discussion and future program planning.

06

Output

  • Questionnaire summary report
  • Keyword and theme extraction
  • Participant concern categories
  • Follow-up communication insight structure
  • Program improvement notes
